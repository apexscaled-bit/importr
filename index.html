FAQ

Common
questions

Is this legal? Can I resell Amazon products?
Yes. You are sourcing products that are listed for sale on Amazon and reselling them at your own price — this is standard dropshipping practice. You are responsible for fulfilling orders from your supplier of choice.
Does Importr handle order fulfillment?
No — Importr handles the product import and listing setup. Fulfillment is managed by you through your chosen supplier. Many users fulfil via Amazon, AliExpress, or a private supplier.
Which Shopify plans does this work with?
Importr works with all Shopify plans including Basic, Shopify, Advanced, and Plus. You just need a Shopify store with product write access.
How does the AI-written copy work?
Importr fetches the product data from Amazon and passes it to our AI which rewrites the title, description, and tags for Shopify. The result is conversion-focused copy rather than Amazon SEO-stuffed text.
Can I import from non-US Amazon stores?
Yes. Importr supports amazon.com, amazon.co.uk, amazon.de, amazon.ca, amazon.com.au and other regional marketplaces. Just paste the product URL from any Amazon site.
What happens when I hit my free import limit?
After 5 free imports you'll be prompted to upgrade to a paid plan. Your existing imported products remain live in your store — you just need to upgrade to continue importing new ones.
