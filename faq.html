Getting started
How do I get started with Importr?
Sign up with your Google account, verify your phone number, then connect your Shopify store via OAuth. Once connected, paste any Amazon product URL to import your first product. The whole setup takes under two minutes.
Do I need a credit card to sign up?
No. The free plan includes 5 product imports with no credit card required. You only need to add payment details when you choose to upgrade to Pro or Business.
Which Shopify plans does Importr work with?
Importr works with all Shopify plans — Basic, Shopify, Advanced, and Plus. You just need a store with product write access enabled, which is included in all plans by default.
Can I connect multiple Shopify stores?
Currently one store per account. Multi-store support is on the roadmap for the Business plan. If this is important to you, let us know.
Importing products
Which Amazon marketplaces are supported?
Importr supports amazon.com, amazon.co.uk, amazon.de, amazon.ca, amazon.com.au, amazon.fr, amazon.it, amazon.co.jp, and more. Just paste the product URL from any supported Amazon site and Importr detects the marketplace automatically.
What product categories can I import?
Any publicly listed Amazon product across all categories — electronics, fashion, home goods, beauty, sports, toys, books and more. If it has a product page on Amazon, Importr can import it.
Does Importr import product variants?
Currently Importr imports the main product listing. Full variant support (sizes, colors etc.) is coming in a future update.
Can I edit the product before it goes live?
Yes. Before anything is pushed to your store you see a review screen where you can set your sale price, compare-at price, override the title, and add tags. You're always in control.
AI-generated content
How does the AI-written copy work?
Importr fetches the product data from Amazon and passes it to our AI model which rewrites the title, description, and tags specifically for Shopify. The output is conversion-focused copy rather than Amazon SEO-stuffed text.
What exactly gets rewritten by AI?
The product title, full description, SEO page title, meta description, and product tags are all AI-generated. Images and pricing data come directly from Amazon and are not altered.
Can I edit the AI copy after importing?
Yes — once the product is in your Shopify store you can edit it like any other product from your Shopify admin. The AI copy is a starting point, not locked in.
Legal & business
Is it legal to resell Amazon products?
Reselling products is legal and is standard practice in dropshipping and retail arbitrage. You are sourcing products available for public purchase and reselling them at your own price. You are responsible for your own compliance with supplier terms and local regulations.
Does Importr handle fulfillment or shipping?
No — Importr handles product listing only. Order fulfillment is your responsibility and is managed through your chosen supplier. Many users fulfill via Amazon, AliExpress, or a private wholesale supplier.
Are images from Amazon copyright-free to use?
Product images are typically owned by the manufacturer or brand. If you are an authorized reseller of the product, using manufacturer images is generally accepted practice. We recommend verifying with your specific supplier. Importr is not a legal advisor.
Account & billing
What happens when I hit my 5 free imports?
You'll be prompted to upgrade to a paid plan. Your existing 5 imported products remain live in your Shopify store — nothing is removed. You just need to upgrade to continue importing new ones.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime with no fees. You keep access until the end of your billing period. After cancellation you revert to the free plan — all your existing imported products stay live in Shopify.
Why is phone verification required?
Phone verification prevents abuse of the free plan. Each phone number can only be used for one Importr account, which keeps the free tier fair for everyone.
