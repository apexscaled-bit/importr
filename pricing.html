Pricing questions
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — cancel anytime from your account. You keep access until the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees.
What happens to my products if I downgrade?
All products already imported into your Shopify store remain live. You just won't be able to import new ones until you upgrade again.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?
The free plan includes 5 imports so you can test the full product before paying. No time limit on the free plan.
Do you charge per import on paid plans?
No. Pro and Business plans include unlimited imports — no per-import charges, no surprises.
